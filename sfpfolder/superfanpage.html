Kendrick Lamar

Kendrick Lamar is an american rapper, song producer, and songwriter. He was born Kendrick Lamar Duckworth on June 17, 1987 in Compton California. Kendrick Lamar embarked on his musical career as a teen under the name K.Dot, releasing a mixtape that gained local attention within his comunity. Kendrick released his first studio album "Section 80"on July 2, 2011. Kendricks albums from section 80 foreward have only gained popularity and sales. Kendrick has also gained a large following of fans with more than 32 million monthly listeners on spotify.
